Dots and Figures

With every click you make, the system highlights its location with a red circle. After three clicks are made, the system draws a parallelogram with those clicks as three of its corners and a circle with the same area and center of mass as the parallelogram. At any time, you can drag any of the click-circles and, as you move it around, the figures will be updated accordingly.
