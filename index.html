Dots and Figures

Description
With every click you make, the system highlights its location with a red circle.
After three clicks, the system draws a blue parallelogram with those points as three of its corners and a circle with the same area and center of mass as the parallelogram.
At any time, you can drag any of the circles and, as you move it around, the figures will be updated accordingly.
